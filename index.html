About Us

We present H2Overflow, the Web-Based Water Usage Tracker where you will be able to set saving goals, input your daily water consumption data and recieve statistics and visualize them to help you monitor and manage your water usage effectively.

The Importance of Saving Water

We present H2Overflow, a web-based Water Usage Tracker. This application will enable users to create an account, set goals, input their daily water consumption data and will provide them with dynamic statistics and visualizations depending on their goals in order to help them monitor and manage their water usage effectively.
